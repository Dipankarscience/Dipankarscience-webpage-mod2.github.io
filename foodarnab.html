Best Biriyani in Kolkata

By Arnab Roy

OUDH 1590-:PRICE-:395/plate(approx) Specialsed in Awadhi Biriyanis,Oudh makes one of the best Biriyanis in Kolkata,They have a great ambience in their outlets and justifying their name they try to give their customers the old era feelings.They are also specialized in kebabs and other mughlai dishes.You can experience Oudh Biriyani in their branches in Salt Lake.Lake Town.
Address 1-:Street number 68,CD block sector-1 Bidhannagar,Kolkata-:700064
Address 2-:Singhs,deshapriya park W,Kalighat,manoharpukur,Kolkata:700026.
Address 3-:P-562 Hemanta mukherjee Sarani,Kolkata-:700029

ARSALAN PARKCIRCUS-:PRICE-:350/plate: Arsalan have now opened their branches all over Kolkata but debatably their best Biriyani can be experienced in their oldest and the first branch situated in the park circus maidan more.Specialise in Biriyani.People are crazy about their Biriyani and Firni combo.The ambience in the restaurant is not that good but the Biriyani is just wesome.They are specialisedin making less oily yet the most delicious Biriyani in Kolkata.
Address 1-:Park circus 7 point crossing,Kolkata-:700017

AMINIA-:PRICE-:PRICE-:350-: Specialised in Biriyani and Chicken Chaap,The most famous combination among the foodies of Kolkata.Their Biriyani is incomplete without their most famous Aminia Chicken Chaap.They also make delicious firnis.They have now made many branches but their oldest branch is in Esplanade.Other branches are in Hatibagan,saltlake,Laketown,nager bazaar etc.
Address 1-:94,Bidhan Sarani Kolkata-:700004
Address 2-:166,Jessore road,Satgachi,Kolkata-:700055.

Image source-:Tripadvisor

DADA BOUDI HOTEL-:PRICE-:320-: Undoubtedly the most rich Biriyani among this lot.Their Biriyany is full of flavours and high richness.Dada Boudi’s Biriyani is just mouth watering with best in class teaste and juicy mutton,They are famous for Biriyanis in whole Bengal.Only drawback is that they have only 2 branches one in Barrackpore and another one in Sodhpur.The Ambience of their restaurants are not that good but believe me when you will take the first bite of their Biriyani,you will forget everything and will be lost in the awesome flavor and teaste of their Biriyani.
Address 1-:1,ghosh para Barrackpore,opposite Barrackpore railway station,Kolkata-:700120
Address 2-:3,deshbandhunagar Sodhpur,Kolkata-:700115

ROYAL INDIAN HOTEL-:PRICE-:350-: The oldest restaurant in Kolkata,Undoubtedly famous for its Biriyani and Chicken Chaap.They have only one branch that is situated in chitpur.They are specialized in making light biriyanis and Awesome chicken chhaps.Their biriyani is incomplete without their Chicken chaaps.They make less oily Biriyani but still manages to make the Biriyani awesome.
Address 1-:147,Rabindra sarani,Chitpur,Kolkata-:700073
